Market-Specific Intelligence

Palestine & Jordan Context

🌍 Regional Disease Priorities

  • Hyperpigmentation + melasma (intense UV, Mediterranean climate)
  • Tinea versicolor & fungal infections (heat + humidity)
  • Atopic dermatitis in children (high prevalence)
  • Vitiligo (high stigma, underdiagnosed)
  • Scalp psoriasis (stress-aggravated in conflict regions)
  • Acne in young adults (ages 18–30 are primary TikTok users)
  • Traction alopecia in hijab-wearing women

📱 Platform Behaviour

  • TikTok is dominant for ages 18–30 in Jordan & Palestine
  • Instagram Reels preferred by 25–40 age group
  • Arabic-language content gets 3–5x more engagement than English-only in the region
  • Bilingual captions (Arabic + English) maximise reach
  • WhatsApp sharing is the #1 off-platform spread vector — make content shareable via link
  • Peak posting times: 8–10pm (post-Isha prayer)

🎯 Content Dos & Don'ts

  • DO address cultural stigma around skin diseases directly
  • DO include Islamic medical ethics perspective where relevant
  • DO show female dermatologists — critical for female patient trust
  • DON'T show overly graphic procedures (cultural sensitivity)
  • DON'T mock traditional remedies — validate, then correct
  • DO use patient testimonials in Arabic — most trusted format
  • DO reference local climate (dry summers, cold winters in Jordan)
